# Lunivo

**Lunivo** is a sleek, youth-centric investment tracker that blends real-time financial insights with elegant, intuitive design. It empowers users to monitor, analyze, and visualize their portfolios with ease and style.

## Features

- **Real-time Insights**: Track your investments and get up-to-date financial data.
- **Elegant UI**: Designed for simplicity and clarity, Lunivo puts usability first.
- **Portfolio Analysis**: Visualize and evaluate your holdings with interactive charts and analytics.
- **Responsive Design**: Optimal experience across desktop and mobile devices.
- **Customizable Tracking**: Monitor stocks, crypto, and more, tailored to your preferences.
